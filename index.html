Simple Omelette Reciepe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese,diced
    vegetables, cooked meat, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are all mixed well. You can add a tabelspoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the buttee is melted or bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly running in the midddle, sprinkle your choice of fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute, the slide it unto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
